PressPlay

For this project, I was tasked with making a website that allowed customers to buy products or subscribe to the website. I chose to create a gaming website that sells CD-Keys utilizing HTML, CSS, JavaScript. The website was implemented in three stages. The first stage was the concept stage in which I sketched various ideas to help ideate through my design process. Once I was satisfied with my design, I created a low-fidelity prototype in skecth and begin demonstrating my various ideas. Before proceeding into the final stage, I conducted various user tests of my prototype to get a better understanding of what users found meanginful, uneeded, or missing.
